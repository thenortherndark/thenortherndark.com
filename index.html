About the Series

The Northern Dark is a supernatural detective comic written and illustrated by Dennis Gustafsson.

Nine graphic novels, together with a handful of shorts and specials, have appeared in various publications since 2010. Having aquired something of a cult following in Sweden, The Northern Dark comic books are currently being expanded and translated into English to be published as digital comics via Comixology.
